About

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a full-stack developer with a focus on JavaScript and MySQL. I received my certification from the University of Minnesota in May 2018, and I am currently open to full-time positions in the Minneapolis/St Paul area, as well as other major metropolitan areas.

I chose a coding "bootcamp" because I started my career in business. I graduated in 2015 from The Ohio State University with a Bachelor's degree in Accounting. Like many accountants fresh out of college, I started my career as an auditor at one of the Big Four firms. After learning the basics of business, I moved into a new role as a Senior Financial Analyst at a local start-up, Bite Squad. Working for a start-up had its challenges, but I loved creating efficient procceses and making a tangible difference on the business's profitability.

After a few months, I realized that I wanted to push myself more. I was working directly with the developers at Bite Squad, and I noticed a gap between the developers and business management. It didn't take long for me to get into coding as a hobby, and I soon realized that I could combine my experiences into a career that I love.

I left my career in finance to spend three months emersing myself into a new world. What you will see within this website is a culmination of the skills I have acquired over the course of the full-stack program. If you have any questions about what you have seen on this website, please send me an email or connect with me on LinkedIn!
